Greenwald density goes like $$ I_p / \pi * a^2 $$.

or like 

\begin{equation}
\label{eq:greenwald}
n_g = \frac{I_p}{\pi a^2}
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
\label{eq:plasma_beta}
\beta = \frac{p}{B^2/2\mu_0}
\end{equation}

going above this limit results in your tokamak exploding!
